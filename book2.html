Twisted Love

Author: Ana Huang

Description:"Twisted Love" is the first book in the Twisted series by Ana Huang, a contemporary romance novel that weaves together passion, heartbreak, and redemption. The story follows Ava Chen, a kind-hearted, optimistic young woman, and Alex Volkov, a brooding, ruthless billionaire who is haunted by a dark past. Ava is a college student with a painful history, but she tries to live life with a positive outlook. When her brother, who is Alex's best friend, leaves the country for work, Alex is asked to look after Ava. Despite his cold and calculating demeanor, Alex finds himself drawn to Ava in ways he never anticipated. As their relationship deepens, dark secrets are revealed, and both characters are forced to confront their pasts. The novel explores themes of love, trust, and the fine line between obsession and passion. With intense chemistry between the protagonists and a mix of suspense and drama, "Twisted Love" offers readers an emotionally charged journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. It's a story of how love can be both healing and destructive, and how two seemingly opposite people can find solace in each other despite the odds.
