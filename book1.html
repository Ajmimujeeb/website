Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Author: J. K. Rowling

Description:"Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban" is the third book in J.K. Rowling's iconic Harry Potter series. The novel follows the young wizard Harry Potter during his third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The story begins with Harry's discovery that a dangerous wizard, Sirius Black, has escaped from the infamous Azkaban prison and is believed to be coming after him. As the year unfolds, Harry learns more about the complex history between Sirius Black and his own family, particularly his late parents, James and Lily Potter. The book introduces new magical elements, including the fearsome Dementors—guards of Azkaban who can drain happiness from anyone near them—and the mystical Marauder's Map, a detailed, magical map of Hogwarts.
