Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban Novel by J. K. Rowling

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban" is the third book in J.K.Rowling's iconic Harry Potter series. The novel follows the young wizard Harry Potter during his third year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
The story begins with Harry's discovery that a dangerous wizard, Sirius Black, has escaped from the infamous Azkaban prison and is believed to be coming after him.

Twisted Love Book by Ana Huang

"Twisted Love" is the first book in the Twisted series by Ana Huang, a contemporary romance novel that weaves together passion, heartbreak, and redemption. The story follows Ava Chen, a kind-hearted, optimistic young woman, and Alex Volkov, a brooding, ruthless billionaire who is haunted by a dark past

The Kite Runner Novel by Khaled Hosseini

"The Kite Runner" by Khaled Hosseini is a powerful and poignant novel that explores themes of guilt, redemption, friendship, and the complex nature of human relationships. Set against the backdrop of a changing Afghanistan, the story follows the life of Amir, a young boy from a wealthy family in Kabul, and his complicated friendship with Hassan, the son of his father's servant.
